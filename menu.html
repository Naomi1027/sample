Menu

We mainly use organic and pesticide-free ingredients, and we are particular about seasonings, so even if you eat a full meal, you will feel light and leave no residue behind. We offer approximately 20 types of dishes and approximately 15 types of drinks. All drinks and herbal teas are handmade! You can also watch the food being prepared on the island kitchen, and no chemical seasonings or additives are used. This is a restaurant surrounded by greenery in a park where producers, staff, and customers connect.
